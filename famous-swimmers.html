Legends of the Pool

Tributes to the athletes who redefined what is humanly possible in the water.

MP

Michael Phelps

The G.O.A.T.

With 28 Olympic medals (23 gold), Phelps is the most decorated Olympian of all time. Known for his butterfly and individual medley dominance.

KL

Katie Ledecky

Distance Queen

Dominated women's distance freestyle for over a decade. Her 800m and 1500m world records are miles ahead of the competition.

MS

Mark Spitz

Munich '72

Won seven gold medals at the 1972 Munich Olympics, setting world records in all seven events. A record that stood for 36 years.

CD

Caeleb Dressel

Sprint King

Known for his explosive start and underwater dolphin kicks. Current world record holder in the 100m Butterfly.

SS

Sarah Sjöström

Sprint Queen

Swedish powerhouse holding multiple world records in freestyle and butterfly sprints. First Swedish woman to win Olympic gold in swimming.

IT

Ian Thorpe

The Thorpedo

Australian legend who dominated the 200m and 400m freestyle in the early 2000s. Known for his 6-beat kick.
